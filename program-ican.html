Overview

Why Study ICAN With NSL

Our ICAN programme is designed for candidates at every stage — from Foundation to Professional Level — combining live and recorded lessons, structured study guides, and CBT mock exams modeled after the actual ICAN exam format. Instructors are experienced chartered accountants who understand exactly what examiners look for.

Requirements

Foundation Level requires a minimum of five credits in relevant subjects including English and Mathematics at SSCE/WAEC/NECO level (or equivalent). Skills and Professional levels require successful completion of preceding levels or recognized exemptions.
