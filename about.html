About Navigation Services Limited

Building Nigeria's Next Generation of Chartered Professionals

Since our founding, we've helped thousands of students achieve their ICAN, ATSWA, ACCA and CISA qualifications through structured, high-quality tuition.

Our Story

A Tuition Centre Built on Results

Navigation Services Limited was established to bridge the gap between classroom theory and the practical, exam-focused training students need to pass professional accounting examinations. What began as a small tuition centre has grown into a full digital learning platform serving students across ICAN, ATSWA, ACCA and CISA programmes.

Our approach combines experienced instructors, structured curricula, computer-based testing, and — more recently — AI-powered study tools, all built around our three core values: Determination, Diligence and Discipline.
