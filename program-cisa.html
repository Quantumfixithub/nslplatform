Overview

Why Study CISA With NSL

NSL's CISA programme covers all five CISA domains with a focus on exam-pattern questions, domain-by-domain review sessions, and CBT-style practice exams. Ideal for IT auditors, security professionals, and risk managers looking to validate their expertise globally.

Requirements

CISA certification requires passing the CISA exam and a minimum of five years of professional information systems auditing, control or security work experience (with some substitutions and waivers available). NSL's tuition focuses on exam preparation; candidates should verify experience requirements with ISACA directly.
